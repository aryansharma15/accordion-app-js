Batman Arkham Intro.

Arkham Asylum

Joker gets himself arrested to get inside Arkham Asylum and incites a riot once there by letting go of all the inmates. It's Batman's job to get everyone back in their cells and beat Joker and spoil his masterplan.

Arkham City

Hugo Strange has initiated a plan named "Protocol 10" in Arkham City. On another hand, Joker is infected and is using the city's pipes to deliver the infection to innocents. Batman must stop both wwith the aid of Catwoman and Robin.

Arkham Origins

Roman Sionis aka Black Mask has put a bounty on Batman's head and multiple superassassins have joined the cause. Who'll get the Bat? Bane, Firefly, or Deathstroke? The Batman origin story.

Arkham Knight

Scarecrow has taken over Gotham and intends to release a powerful fear toxin in the entire city. He has the help of a new villain who calls himself the Arkham Knight. Can Batman stop him before it's too late, or will he finally let the "demons" inside his head take over?
